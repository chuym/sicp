Excecise 2.26 - List Operations

Suppose we define x and y to be two list: 

        (define x (list 1 2 3))
        (define y (list 4 5 6))
What is the result printed by the interpreter in response to evaluating each of the following expressions:
  1. (append x y)
    (1 2 3 4 5 6)
  2. (cons x y)
    ((1 2 3) 4 5 6)
  3. (list x y)
    ((1 2 3) (4 5 6))
